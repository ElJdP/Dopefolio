FMP Library

Reflection of my personal identity to represent my love for reading, quiet spaces and studying.

Live Link

Project Overview

The concept of this project was to design a personal interior/architecture space that reflects on our character/personality. I chose to create a library/bookshop, it focuses on helping to prevent the spread deforestation and how much impact it Is having on our planet. To do this my proposal was to sell used books and donate a percentage to a charity that works towards stopping deforestation. A café area was added, this was to add more personal touches. I also focused on using sustainable materials for the café, furniture and flooring. My process included extensive research on deforestation; what is causing it and the damage it is doing. Adding my own personal details made this project individualised and characteristic, which is exactly what the brief asked for.
